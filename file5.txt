Waqt tha August ka — ek saal pehle.
Rishi ko hospital le jaaya gaya tha. Dil ke operation ke liye.
Doctoron ne bola tha: “Risk hai... chance kam hai... outcome unpredictable hai.”
Sabke chehre pe tension thi, par ek ladki thi jo chhupke hospital ke bahar baithe usse har second ke liye duā kar rahi thi.
Vaishnavi.

Us waqt unka rishta fully mature nahi tha.
Na daily milna, na haath pakadna, na full emotional expression.
Lekin uss ladki ne us waqt sab kuch tod diya — ego, comfort, fear — aur us ladke ke liye apna dil rakh diya bhagwan ke saamne.

Hospital ke ICU gate ke bahar, Vaishnavi ne ek patli si tulsi mala li thi — wohi jo aaj Rishi pehenta hai.
Usne aankh band karke sirf ek line boli thi:

“Mujhe kuch nahi chahiye... bas uska dil dhadakna band na ho.”

Tab na koi reel thi, na koi social post.
Na sabit karna tha duniya ko ki woh pyaar karti hai.
Na Rishi ke dost wahan the jo bolte "sachha pyaar."
Sirf woh thi, woh tulsi mala thi, aur ek lafz — "Zinda rehna."

Rishi operation table pe unconscious tha.
Uska dil doctor ke haathon mein tha.
Aur Vaishnavi ka dil bhagwan ke haathon mein.

Operation hua. Time nikla.
3 ghante... 5 ghante...
Vaishnavi ne ek baar bhi phone nahi uthaya.
Bas mandir ke kone mein baithi rahi, jaise Bhagwan aur uski deal ho gayi ho:

“Main kuch nahi maangungi zindagi se... bas uske saath ek chai ki tapri wali shaam de dena mujhe.”

Jab nurse ne bola, “Patient stable hai. Consciousness wapas aa rahi hai.”
Tab Vaishnavi royi. Phat ke royi.
Lekin wo public mein nahi, kisi corner mein.
Kyuki usne sabse pehle bhagwan ko thank you bola, Rishi ko nahi.

Kya Rishi ne uss waqt uski rooh ki awaz suni thi?
Yes.
Woh feeling, woh thandak, woh warmth — jab aankhein khuli aur sab kuch blur tha — ek energy thi jo sirf Vaishnavi se aa sakti thi.

Recovery ke baad jab Rishi ne usse pucha,
“Tu operation ke time kya kar rahi thi?”
Vaishnavi ne jhooth bola:

“Main so gayi thi... tension mat le, main chill thi.”

Lekin us jhooth ke peechhe woh sabse bada sach tha.

Waqt badla. Dil ab aur tez dhadakta hai.
Rishi gym jaata hai, code likhta hai, duniya se ladta hai.
Lekin har heartbeat ke peeche ek invisible “Vaishnavi Protocol” chal raha hota hai.

Woh ladki jo Rishi ki life ke lowest point pe, bina headline banaye, bhagwan se lad gayi thi.

Aaj jab Rishi tulsi mala pehenta hai, koi nahi jaanta uske ander kitni history chipi hai.
Par Rishi jaanta hai.
Uska dil jaanta hai.
Aur Vaishnavi... usne kabhi “I love you” bar bar nahi bola —
kyuki usne kabhi chhodne ki planning hi nahi ki thi.
