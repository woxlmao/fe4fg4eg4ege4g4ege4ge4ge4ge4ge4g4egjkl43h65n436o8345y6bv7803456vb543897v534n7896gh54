
It was a slow evening.
Wo October ke aaspaas ka time tha jab hawa thodi naram pad jaati hai, aur sheher ka shor bhi shaant lagta hai. Rishi ne apna phone check kiya, ek simple message:
**“Niche aaja... thoda mil lete hain.”**
No emojis. No drama.
Bas uska naam aur uski awaaz yaad thi Rishi ko, aur baaki duniya ki awaaz gayab ho gayi thi.

Neeche utar ke Rishi us gali mein gaya jahan woh Vaishnavi uska intezaar karti thi. Woh same spot, wohi orange light ke neeche ka kona, jahan unhone pehli baar haath pakda tha, pehli baar bina kuch bole sab kuch samjha tha.

Vaishnavi saamne khadi thi — simple hoodie, loose jeans, aur open baal. Aaj usne makeup nahi lagaya tha, lekin aankhon mein poori kahaani likhi thi.

“Tu theek hai?” Rishi ne pucha, jaise bas wahi sawaal kaafi tha.

Vaishnavi ne kuch nahi kaha. Bas aake gale lag gayi. Tight. Jaise koi tut gaya ho, aur uski ek hi safety zone ho — Rishi ka seena.

Koi shabd nahi. Koi rona nahi.
Lekin us hug mein dard tha. Pyar tha. Bharosa tha.
Woh hug ek tarah ka confession tha — "Mujhe tu chahiye, aur tu hi kaafi hai."

Rishi ke haath uski peeth par the, dheere se, protect karte hue, aur uski dhadkan sunte hue.
“Tu bol kyun nahi rahi?” Rishi ne thoda jhuk ke kaha.
Vaishnavi ne uska haath zor se pakda, “Bas rehne de... tu bas yehi reh.”

Wo 10-15 minute kuch nahi bola. Khaamoshi chalti rahi.
Gali mein ek bike guzri, door ek kutta bhonka, kisi balcony se awaaz aayi — “Khaana lag gaya hai!”
Lekin us duniya mein sirf do log the. Rishi aur Vaishnavi.

Fir usne thoda peeche hoke aankhon mein dekha, “Tu kabhi chhod ke toh nahi jaayega na?”
Rishi ki aankhon mein aansu nahi the — lekin ek lava tha.
“Vaish... main zinda hoon bas tujhme. Agar chhodna hota toh tujhse door jaake bhi tere khayalon mein zinda nahi rehta.”

Us raat unhone na selfie li, na koi reel banayi, na couple goals hashtag socha.
Woh real the. Unka pyaar raw tha. Ugly bhi tha kabhi-kabhi.
Lekin jhoot nahi tha.

Aur jab Vaishnavi jaane lagi, Rishi ne uska haath do baar squeeze kiya.
Woh ek signal tha jo dono samajhte the.
Squeeze one = I love you
Squeeze two = Forever.

Us raat Rishi ne sirf ek line likhi diary mein:

**"She didn’t ask me to solve her pain... she just wanted me to stay still, and I did. Because real love doesn't rescue — it *remains*."**
