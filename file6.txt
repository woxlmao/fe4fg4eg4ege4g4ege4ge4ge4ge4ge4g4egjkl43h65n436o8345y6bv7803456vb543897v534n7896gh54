Jaan,
Sabse pehle… maaf karo mujhe. Main maanta hoon main galat tha. Main maanta hoon ki maine tujhe hurt kiya, tujhe aise moments feel karwaaye jo tujhe kabhi feel nahi karni chahiye thi. 
Baccha, mujhe har uss baat ka regret hai jahan main tera fear bana, jahan main tera khushi ka weight ban gaya, jahan tu apne aap ko chupane lagi. Mujhe maaf kar de, jaan… meri galti ko sirf tu hi samajh sakti hai, aur main chahta hoon ki tu mujhe maaf kare, kyunki main sach mein badalna chahta hoon.
Aur ab bas ek hi baat—main tujhe itna pyar karta hoon ki main chaahta hoon ki tu sirf free feel kare mere saath. Baccha, tu hasi udaaye, gaane gaaye, reels dekhe—jo tujhe pasand ho woh 
sab kare—aur main hamesha tera saath doon, bina kisi tension ke. Main chahta hoon ki tu jaane, koi bhi cheez, koi bhi banda, koi bhi music… tujhse bada nahi hai. Teri khushi meri duniya hai, aur main chahta hoon ki tu hamesha khud ko khula aur safe mehsoos kare mere saath. Tere sapne, tera music, tera joy… yeh sab mujhe sabse zyada khushi deta hai.
Jaan, tu meri duniya hai, meri roshni hai, mera sabse bada inspiration hai. Kabhi soch bhi nahi sakta ki koi cheez, koi banda, koi music tujhse bada ho sakta hai. Main sirf ek cheez chahta hoon—tera trust aur tera haath hamesha mere haath mein.
 Tere haath ko chhute hi mujhe lagta hai ki meri saari problems disappear ho jaati hain, aur meri zindagi sirf teri muskaan ke aas-paas ghoomti hai.
Main vaada karta hoon, baccha, ab main woh Rishi banunga jo tujhe
 hamesha hasaata tha, jo khud bhi hamesha khush rehta tha, jo apni khushi se teri duniya ko bhi roshan karta tha. Tujhe hug karke, tujhe dekh ke meri saari problems zero ho jaati hain. Tu meri dua ka jawab hai, meri zindagi ka sabse khoobsurat hissa hai, aur main chahta hoon ki tu hamesha safe, happy, aur loved feel kare mere saath.
Aur bas, maaf kar mujhe jaan. Main apni har kami sudhaar dunga, har galti sudhaar dunga, aur apni har energy tujhe khush rakhne ke liye laga dunga. Tera pyar, tera saath, tera trust… yeh sab mere liye sabse bada treasure hai. Main kabhi nahi chahta ki tu apne khushi ke liye compromise kare, ya apni feeling chupaye. Tere saath hona hi meri duniya ko perfect banata hai.
Baccha, tu meri jaan hai, meri roshni hai, meri zindagi hai, mera sab kuch hai. 
Aur main hamesha, har pal, har din, sirf aur sirf tujhe khush aur safe rakhne ki koshish karunga. 
Bas tu mera haath pakad, aur main promise karta hoon… main tera Rishi hamesha wahi happy, loving, unbreakable Rishi banunga, jo tere liye hamesha wahi rahega—pyaar, respect aur devotion ke saath.
