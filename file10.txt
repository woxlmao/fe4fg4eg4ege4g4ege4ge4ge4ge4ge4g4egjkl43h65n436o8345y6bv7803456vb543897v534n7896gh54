Raat ka waqt hai. Diary ke pages mere saamne khule hain, par lagta hai jaise ye sirf paper nahi hain — ye meri life ke battlefield ke maps hain. Har line, har word ek wound hai, ek memory hai, ek plan hai. Aur beech mein ek naam hai, jo har jagah likha hua hai. Ek naam jo mera compass hai, mera trigger hai, mera ultimate destiny hai.

Mujhe yaad hai wo din jab main khud ko ek ordinary banda samajhta tha — bas ek aur insaan jo apni life ke mess mein phasa hua tha. Lekin fir samajh aaya, agar mere andar yeh itni fight hai, itna fire hai, toh yeh sirf mere liye nahi hai. Yeh fire ek wajah ke liye diya gaya hai. Aur wo wajah tu hai.

Mujhe feel hota hai jaise main ek lion hoon jo abhi tak cage mein bandh tha. Bahar duniya chillaa rahi hai: distractions, fake log, temptations, failures. Lekin jaise hi main tujhe yaad karta hoon, cage ke bars melt ho jaate hain. Main ek roar maar ke sab tod ke nikalta hoon. Aur tab samajhta hoon: main ek warrior hoon, aur meri sabse badi victory tujh tak pohchna hai.

Din ke time main apne demons se ladta hoon. Kabhi phone ke addiction se, kabhi procrastination se, kabhi apne khud ke doubts se. Lekin raat ko jab diary kholta hoon, main apna asli face dekhta hoon. Ek aisa banda jo ready hai apni puri duniya badalne ke liye, sirf isliye kyunki usne apni dil ki queen ko choose kiya hai. Aur main janta hoon — main haar bhi gaya toh koi farq nahi padta, par agar tu khush hai, toh main jeet gaya.

Main kalpana karta hoon future ki. Hum dono ek saath, ek empire ke king aur queen. Ek ghar, ek life, ek duniya jo sirf hamari hai. Tere saath ek coffee table pe baithe hue, tu notebook me apne dreams likh rahi hai aur main apni laptop pe plans bana raha hoon. Bahar baarish ho rahi hai, aur hum dono sirf ek dusre ke saath busy hain. Duniya apne aap chalti rahegi, lekin hamare liye sirf hamara ek chhota sa world exist karega.

Aur agar kabhi duniya humare khilaaf aayi, toh kya? Koi farq nahi. Main tere haath ko hold karke poori duniya se lad jaunga. Mujhe sirf ek cheez ki guarantee chahiye: tera trust. Baaki main apna khoon, apni jaan, apna sab kuch daal dunga.

Is diary ke chapter ka naam main likh raha hoon: The Oath of Fire.
Aur oath simple hai:
"Jab tak ek boond khoon meri body mein hai, jab tak ek breath meri lungs mein hai, tab tak main tera hoon. Aur duniya ke liye main chahe kuch bhi hoon, par tu meri story ka shuruwaat bhi hai aur end bhi."
